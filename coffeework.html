10 jobs with haviest coffee drinkers

Coffee is a traditional work beverage. You drink it when you need to get that kick when the afternoon sleepiness starts to set in. But some jobs have to energize more than the other. What do you think do you belong to the 10 most caffeinated workplaces?

10. Financial Professional

You know your coffee as you know your taxes. Well who would be surprised considering you have to pull frequent all nighters to get all the math and papers done for the clients. In these situation good ol’ cup of Joe is your best friend to keep you on your feet and numbers.

9. Social worker

No matter how much you like people everybody needs a break to recharge the battery from time to time. Of course if you need to get more social there are other beverages than coffee for that but if you don’t wanna get fired coffee is your best option.

8. Professor

The heights of education can prove treacherous if you lack the energy to manage them. Good thing that cup of black one will be always there for you to motivate you for another day dealing with students and colleagues. Shame only it cannot write research papers for you.

7. Food preparer

Chefs have some of the harshest hours to work and many of us don’t realize it. They are first to arrive to heat up the owens and start preparing food and stay the latest to clean up the kitchen. No wonder they need coffee breaks while the rest of us work so they can be up and running full speed when the whole town collectively takes a lunch break.

6. Physician

Again taking care of people is no walk through the park especially when about once a day one of those people comes in with the googled disease (probably cancer) to their cold symptoms. We can’t blame those poor doctors getting all up on the liquid energy coffee offers to solve our numerous problems and pains.

5. Healthcare administrator

As mentioned before the healthcare industry can be very stressful and lead to high energy demands. Especially if you have to work overtime as many of them do. Really no surprise these stressful and utmost vital jobs for our society consume so much of our coffee supply.

3. Education administrator

Kids, a joy, unless you have to teach them algebra. The small war going on in classroom can cause lot of stress and cost a lot of energy. No surprise teachers non dominant hands are demoted to cup holders for most of their workday.

2.Marketing/PR Proffesional

Second place holders just by the title have jobs where stress and business come together to create a coffee drinkers of incredible level. Every day racing against customers and competitors is a but coffee can help lift that heavy burden.

1. Scientist/Lab technical

Gold medal goes to the lab crawlers. Indeed a stressful and uncertain line of job. Requires lot of hope. Hope your hypothesis will be proven, hope to be published, hope that you will get the grant. While they hope and work hard coffee is there to keep them from despair and possible plans to destroy the world (depending what kind of lab you work in).
